About

Public health at the intersection of data, feasibility, operations, and equity.

My work has focused on how public health interventions are implemented, evaluated, and sustained in complex delivery environments. I bring experience in research, program management, stakeholder engagement, systematic and scoping reviews, qualitative and mixed-methods analysis, multilingual survey implementation, and GIS-based analysis.

Across academic, nonprofit, healthcare, and community settings, I have supported projects in the United States, India, Latin America and the Caribbean, and the MENA region, with a strong interest in making evidence useful for practice, policy, and community impact.
